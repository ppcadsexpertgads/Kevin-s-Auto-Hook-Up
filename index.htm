Paint correction

 As part of our detailing services we also offer paint correcting to further enhance your vehicles paint. Paint correcting is used to eliminate blemishes and clear some scratching from the vehicles surface and can go from 1 to 3 different stages of correcting!

Ceramic Coating

Our ceramic coating service offers a whole new feel to your vehicles exterior by providing a glossy and slick new look to your vehicles exterior! Ceramic coating is not like your typical wax/sealant, it’s a hydrophobic shield that further enhances and protects your vehicle from harmful UV sun rays, weather elements, etc.

Our window tint technician with 10+ years of experience works to ensure that you are provided with quality work and the correct film to meet your tinting needs.
